QUALIFICATIONS

The Shrek-Juices brand is continuously looking for multi-unit operators with partners who have extensive restaurant experience and are committed to providing an excellent guest experience. The Shrek-Juices new franchisee approval process includes: Completion of the Franchise Application to understand professional background and personal finances Verification of net worth and liquid assets through a CPA opinion letter Submission of Business Plan outlining proposed organization structure, operations plan, marketing plan and growth and acquisition plan Please note that these qualifications apply to franchisees applying for the continental US market. Qualifications may change based on your country of application.
